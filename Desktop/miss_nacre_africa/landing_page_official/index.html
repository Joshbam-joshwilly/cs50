MORE ABOUT US

Miss Nacre Africa is a social enterprise that focuses on empowering young women to join leadership roles and decision-making with the aim of encouraging inclusivity and equality for all in Rwanda. Our work is done through mentorship, skills development, leadership, and entrepreneurial programs to enhance sustainable development goals for African communities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a platform for young women to learn and obtain resources as well as effective support that enables them to become leaders of change within their communities through innovative solutions.

Our Project

Act To Impact

Miss Nacre Africa conducted a philanthropic campaign called ATI (Act To Impact) on 18th December 2021. The activity that targeted to support 60 young women in Masaka District Hospital. We reached young women between 60-80 in number in neonatology department with hygienic/sanitary requirements, paid hospital bills for vulnerable, it was an impactful social activity that we aim to keep on doing in series.

Build In A box

African Leadership Academy (ALA) seeks to support ALA students who would like to run entrepreneurial leadership camps across Africa through BUILD-in-a-Box (BIAB); a portable toolkit that provides content and teaching materials that allow a team of facilitators to run a professional TWO DAY Entrepreneurial Leadership camp. The curriculum is based on BUILD, a unique curriculum framework for teaching youth entrepreneurship as a way of fighting unemployment and engaging youth to lead solutions to local problems.
